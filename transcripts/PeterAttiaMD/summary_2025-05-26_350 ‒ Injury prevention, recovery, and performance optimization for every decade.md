## Description

Kyler Brown is a sports rehab chiropractor who specializes in injury recovery, pre- and post-surgical rehabilitation, & guiding clients from rehab back to peak performance. As co-founder of the private training program 10 Squared alongside Peter, Kyler shares his approach to injury prevention & performance optimization—highlighting how overlooked movement patterns can quietly lead to chronic issues over time. He outlines a practical framework for assessing & treating individuals, highlights the importance of understanding movement dynamics & asymmetries, & discusses personalized rehab strategies through real-world case studies. The conversation also explores the psychological side of recovery, including the role of fear & mental barriers in rehabilitation. Kyler shares actionable tools to evaluate movement patterns & implement training strategies that support long-term strength & health.
