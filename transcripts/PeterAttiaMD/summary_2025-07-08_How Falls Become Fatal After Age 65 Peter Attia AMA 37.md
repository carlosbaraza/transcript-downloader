In this clip, they discuss:

- Top Causes of Accidental Death by Age – A CDC-based analysis reveals falls as a leading cause of accidental death in those over 65

- Falls vs. Other Major Diseases – Deaths from falls rank just behind Alzheimer's, cancer, and heart disease in older adults.
