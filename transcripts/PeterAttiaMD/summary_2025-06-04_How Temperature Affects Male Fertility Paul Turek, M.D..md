In this clip, they discuss:

- Heat Exposure Affects Male Fertility – The testicles are temperature-sensitive, and excess heat can interfere with sperm production
-  Hot Tubs Are a Known Risk Factor – Time spent immersed in hot water can negatively impact sperm quality and fertility
- Saunas May Be Less Concerning Than Hot Tubs – Because they don’t heat the scrotum as directly, saunas might be safer, though caution is still advised
