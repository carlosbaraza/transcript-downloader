In this clip, they discuss:

- Common Toe Injuries & Causes - Chronic ankle sprains and poor footwear often contribute to limited mobility in the big toe
-  Why Toe Mobility Matters - You need 40–45° of big toe motion for efficient walking—65° for sprinting
- Turf Toe & Hallux Limitus - NFL players and others suffer from turf toe, leading to joint inflammation and long-term pain
