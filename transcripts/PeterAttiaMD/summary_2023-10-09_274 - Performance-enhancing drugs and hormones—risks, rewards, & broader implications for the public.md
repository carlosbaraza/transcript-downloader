Derek is a fitness educator & the entrepreneur behind More Plates More Dates & an expert in molecules commonly used & misused by bodybuilders & athletes. This conversation explores various compounds like growth hormone, testosterone, androgens, SARMs, estrogen, finasteride, peptides, & much more. Derek walks through how these molecules are used by athletes to gain an edge while also exploring their impact on disease risk, hair loss, fertility, & more. This conversation aims to provide insights into whether these compounds are uniformly harmful or if there are broader implications that could benefit the general population.
