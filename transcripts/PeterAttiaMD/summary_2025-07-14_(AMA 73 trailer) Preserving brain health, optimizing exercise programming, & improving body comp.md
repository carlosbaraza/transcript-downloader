## Description

In this “Ask Me Anything” (AMA) episode, Peter addresses frequently requested topics, including brain health, exercise programming, & body composition. He explores the factors that influence dementia risk—including metabolic health, fitness, sleep, hearing, cholesterol levels, & more. He explains how to implement zone 2 training & VO₂ max intervals for cardiovascular fitness & also covers how to combine cardio modalities, how to stay in zone 2, & special considerations for women. Peter highlights the benefits of light movement after meals & offers strength training tips for those managing injuries. Additional insights include how to maintain fat loss, the truth about so-called “slow metabolisms,” & how to set appropriate daily protein goals while managing the trade-off between lean mass & body fat over time.
