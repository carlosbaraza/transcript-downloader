Mark Rosekind is an expert on road safety & a policy leader with more than 30 years of experience enacting strategic, practical, & effective data-based solutions that enhance driver & pedestrian safety & health in complex environments. In this episode, Mark delves into the persistent issue of accidental deaths resulting from roadway accidents, a concern for those focused on longevity given its consistent risk throughout life. From exploring statistics on car crashes to identifying the demographics most at risk & the key locations of incidents, he uncovers various risk factors including distractions like smartphone usage, the influence of alcohol & cannabis, the dangers of sleep deprivation, & speeding. Mark also provides practical advice for both drivers & pedestrians to enhance safety, while delving into the potential & challenges of emerging technologies such as autonomous vehicles. Mark provides valuable resources for listeners, particularly parents navigating the road safety landscape with teenage drivers.
