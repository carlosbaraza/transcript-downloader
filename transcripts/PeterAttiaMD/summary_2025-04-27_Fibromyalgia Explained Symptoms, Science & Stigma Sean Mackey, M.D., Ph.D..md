In this clip, they discuss:

- What Is Fibromyalgia? – A syndrome of widespread pain, fatigue, sleep disturbance, and cognitive issues, historically misunderstood
- We Still Don’t Fully Understand It – Fibromyalgia is defined by symptoms, not a known cause, but brain and nerve dysfunctions are suspected
- From Tender Points to New Criteria – Diagnostic standards have evolved to focus on symptom severity and widespread pain rather than specific points
