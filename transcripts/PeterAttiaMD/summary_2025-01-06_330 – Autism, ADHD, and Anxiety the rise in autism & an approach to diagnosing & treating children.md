Trenna Sutcliffe is a developmental behavioral pediatrician & the founder & medical director of the Sutcliffe Clinic in the San Francisco Bay Area, where she partners with families to provide care for children facing behavioral challenges, developmental differences, & school struggles. In this episode, Trenna shares her journey into developmental & behavioral pediatrics, including her pioneering work at Stanford & her expertise in autism, ADHD, & anxiety—the "three As." She explores the diagnostic processes, the overlap & comorbidities of these conditions, & the importance of personalized treatment plans that address both medical & environmental factors. Trenna offers valuable insights into the changing prevalence of autism, the impact of evolving diagnostic criteria, & the range of therapies & medications available to support children & their families. She also discusses the challenges in accessing care & the critical need for a holistic approach that bridges healthcare & education.
